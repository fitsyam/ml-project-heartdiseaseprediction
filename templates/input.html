Input Data for Prediction

Form Labels Explanation

Age: Enter your age in years.

Sex: Select your gender.

Chest Pain Type: Select your chest pain type.

Blood Pressure: Enter your blood pressure in mmHg.

Cholesterol: Enter your cholesterol in mg/dl.

FBS Over 120: Select if your fasting blood sugar is over 120 mg/dl.

EKG Results: Enter your EKG results.

Max HR: Enter your maximum heart rate.

Exercise Angina: Select if you have exercise induced angina.

ST Depression: Enter your ST depression induced by exercise relative to rest.

Slope of ST: Enter your slope of the peak exercise ST segment.

Number of Vessel Fluro: Enter your number of major vessels colored by fluro.

Thallium: Enter your thallium stress test result.
